Finternat: Empowering the Next Generation Through Financial Literacy

Our Mission

Finternat is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to improving financial literacy among students and young adults worldwide. Our mission is to make finance accessible, practical, and engaging for everyone. Regardless of background or experience. We believe that by teaching young people how to manage money, invest intelligently, and understand the economy, we can help build a generation that is more financially independent, confident, and capable of creating long-term stability.

What We've Done

Since our founding, Finternat has focused on bringing financial education directly to students through interactive lessons, workshops, and digital learning programs.

Impact so far:

  • 500+ students reached through workshops, online courses, and outreach events.
  • 100+ volunteer hours dedicated to financial education and mentorship.
  • Hosted 12+ online and in-person sessions covering topics like budgeting, investing, credit, and entrepreneurship.
  • Partnered with 3 schools and youth organizations to implement beginner-friendly finance modules.
  • Received positive feedback from 90% of participants, who reported increased confidence in personal finance and investing basics.

What We Offer

Finternat provides a range of free, accessible financial literacy programs designed to met students where they are:

  1. Finance 101: Core lessons on saving, budgeting, and financial decision-making.
  2. Stock Market 101: A gamified learning experience that teaches students how to research, evaluate, and invest in stocks responsibly.
  3. Economics 101: Introductory lessons on how the economy works and how global trends affect personal finance.
  4. Interactive Activities: Simulated stock trading games, budgeting challenges, and entrepreneurship projects.
  5. Workshops & Webinars: Live and virtual events led by youth mentors and finance enthusiasts.
  6. Student Leadership Program: Opportunities for students to become campus ambassadors and spread financial literacy in their schools.

Our Future Goals

Finternat is expanding its impact and reach. In the coming year, we plan to:

  • Reach 1,500+ students globally through virtual courses and collaborations.
  • Launch a mobile app to make our financial literacy courses more interactive and gamified.
  • Develop certification programs for students who complete multiple levels of financial learning.
  • Build partnerships with schools, youth nonprofits, and financial institutions to bring our curriculum to more classrooms.
  • Train student leaders across multiple states to become peer educators in financial literacy.
